Bodyparts For Foreigners

Who are We

Bodyparts For Foreigners Is a Dutch foundation that helps people in Latin America and Africa get prosthetic and/or other aid devices.

We ask everyone via social media, other media, acquaintances, friends, family, businesses and agencies to make donations in the form of a one-off payment, a monthly contribution and/or donating discarded prosthetic and/or other aid devices. If you would like to donate a prosthesis or other aid device please contact us, we will come and collect it or let you know where the items can be sent to. See under contact. Or Become a "Best Friend Forever" and transfer a donation to our account, or authorise us to collect a monthly contribution. Help us put a smile on people's faces, and change the lives of people with disabilities.
